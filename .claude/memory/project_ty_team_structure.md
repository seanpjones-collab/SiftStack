# Team Structure & Sequential Marketing (Ty's Official DataSift Curriculum)

Source: `tmp/datasift_learn/team_structure.md`

## The Deal Flow Machine

"Most investors hire when they are drowning. Someone quits, leads pile up, deals slip through. So they panic-hire and hope it works. That is how you burn $5,000 a month on a team that produces nothing."

"Your team is not a cost center. It is a revenue multiplier. But only if each piece connects to the next. Think of it as a production line: data comes in one end, closed deals come out the other. **Seven stations. Each one does exactly one thing. Skip a station and the line stops.**"

Pipeline: Data → Manager → Callers → Prospectors → Lead Manager → Closer → Deals Out. Owner sets strategy. Sales Mgr / Ops Mgr provide oversight at scale.

**Ty's Tip:** "Your team is only as strong as your weakest link. If your data is dirty, it does not matter how good your callers are. They are burning minutes on wrong numbers. Fix the data first, then add volume."

### 1. Owner / Acquisitions
**Daily Workflow (Solo Phase):**
- Pull first-to-market courthouse data
- Make 150+ outbound calls (click-to-dial via smrtPhone)
- Log leads and dispositions in DataSift CRM
- Follow up on warm and hot leads
- Comp properties and make offers
- Coordinate direct mail via Sift Mail

**Tools:** DataSift CRM, smrtPhone, SiftMap, Sift Mail

**How Your Role Evolves:**
- Hire Data Manager: stop pulling data, skip tracing, mail coordination
- Hire Callers: stop making outbound dials
- Hire Lead Manager: stop qualifying and following up on inbound leads
- Hire Closer: stop making offers and negotiating contracts

### 2. Data Manager ($500–$700/mo)
"The foundation of the entire machine. Dirty data means wasted dials, wasted mail, and wasted money."

**Daily Workflow:**
- 9:00 AM: Pull fresh courthouse records (probate, tax sale, pre-foreclosure)
- 10:00 AM: Run skip trace batches ($0.15/record or $97/mo unlimited)
- 11:00 AM: Trestle phone scoring (Dial First 81-100, Dial Second 61-80, Dial Third 41-60, Dial Fourth 21-40, Drop 0-20)
- 1:00 PM: Trigger Sift Mail (handwritten mailers on Day 1 of niche cycle)
- 2:00 PM: Deep prospecting prep (compile exhausted-lead lists for L2-L3 research)
- 3:00 PM: Filter management in DataSift
- 4:00 PM: Database hygiene

**Tools:** DataSift CRM, Trestle, Sift Mail, Claude, Skip Genie

**KPIs:** 50+ Records/Day, 95%+ Skip Accuracy, 0 Missed Mailings, Daily Filter Updates

**Hire From:** Philippines (strong English, detail-oriented, $500-$700/mo)

"25-100 correct phone numbers = 1 deal. One deal pays for 6-12 months of this role. Without clean data, every dollar on callers is wasted."

### 3. Prospector / Caller ($750–$1,000/mo)
"Volume with precision. 150+ dials a day. Non-negotiable."

**Daily Workflow:**
- 9:00 AM: Review today's call list from Data Manager
- 9:15 AM: Start dials (smrtPhone click-to-dial for niche, ReadyMode for bulk)
- All Day: 150+ dials minimum. Leave voicemail on EVERY attempt. Send a different text after each attempt.
- Ongoing: Log every dial in Tally Counter Chrome extension
- Ongoing: Route warm leads to Lead Manager immediately
- 4:30 PM: Disposition cleanup

**Tools:** smrtPhone, ReadyMode, DataSift CRM, Tally Counter

**KPIs:** 150+ Dials/Day, 32:1 Dials per Correct #, 100% VM + Text Rate, Same Day Lead Handoff

**Hire From:** Latin America: Mexico, Ecuador, Colombia ($750-$1,000/mo)

"32 dials = 1 correct number. At 150 dials/day, that is roughly 5 correct numbers per day."

### 4. Lead Manager ($1,500–$3,000/mo)
"The gatekeeper between raw conversations and closed deals. Qualifies leads using the 4 Pillars of Motivation."

**Daily Workflow:**
- 9:00 AM: Check inbound leads from overnight
- 9:30 AM: Hot lead follow-ups (1-2 day cadence)
- 10:00 AM: Qualification calls using 4 Pillars (Reason, Timeline, Condition, Price). 2+ pillars = hot. 1 = warm. 0 = cold.
- 11:00 AM: Book appointments for qualified hot leads with Closer
- 1:00 PM: Warm lead follow-ups (15-day cadence)
- 2:00 PM: Cold lead cycling (45-day cadence)
- 3:00 PM: Push-back loop (re-contact ghosted leads with temperature preserved)
- 4:00 PM: CRM updates and next-day prep

**Tools:** DataSift CRM, smrtPhone

**KPIs:** 50 Attempts/Day, 3-5 Appts Booked/Wk, 20-30% Not-Int. Recovery, 100% Next Step Logged

**Hire From:** US-based or experienced VA ($1,500-$3,000/mo). "Must handle real conversations with sellers."

"20-30% of all platform deals come from not-interested follow-ups."

### 5. Closer ($2,000 base + 3–10% commission)
"Turns qualified appointments into signed contracts. Consultative, not aggressive."

**Daily Workflow:**
- 9:00 AM: Review today's appointments from Lead Manager
- 10:00-12:00 PM: Seller meetings and calls (consultative)
- 1:00 PM: Comp analysis (SiftMap, Market Finder, or manual)
- 2:00 PM: Prepare written offers and purchase contracts
- 3:00 PM: Negotiation follow-ups
- 4:00 PM: Deal pipeline review with Lead Managers

**Tools:** DataSift CRM, SiftMap, Market Finder

**KPIs:** 5+ Offers/Week, 20%+ Close Rate, $10K+ Avg Deal Spread, <14 Days to Close

**Hire From:** US-based, experienced negotiator ($2,000 base + 3-10% commission)

"Ratio: **2 Lead Managers per 1 Closer.** A closer producing 2 deals a month at $15K spread pays for themselves and the entire team feeding them."

### 6. Sales Manager ($5,000–$6,000/mo + 2-5% override)
"Only needed at scale (3+ callers, 2+ closers). Coaches the team. Does not close deals."

**Responsibilities:**
- Train and onboard new callers and closers
- Review recorded calls and provide coaching feedback
- Monitor daily KPIs: dials, correct numbers, conversations, close rates
- Hold weekly accountability meetings
- Report to owner on daily/weekly performance metrics
- Ensure SOP adherence

**KPIs:** 150+ Per Caller/Day, 20%+ Team Close Rate, Low Team Turnover, Weekly Performance Review

### 7. Operations Manager ($5,000–$7,000/mo + bonus)
"The role that removes you from the business entirely. Only hire when you have multiple closers, a sales manager, and consistent deal flow."

**Responsibilities:**
- Build and maintain SOPs for every role
- Manage CRM data hygiene at scale (thousands of records/month)
- Oversee tool integrations and automations (Zapier, sequences, drip campaigns)
- Handle vendor relationships
- Manage tech stack budget
- Project management for rehabs and complex deals

**KPIs:** 99%+ Data Accuracy, 0 Missed Mailings, 100% SOP Coverage, Monthly Tech Audit

"This hire only makes sense when your business is doing 5+ deals/month and you want to step back from operations."

## The Replacement Ladder

| Rung | Cost/mo | Feeling |
|---|---|---|
| You Today | $0 | Stuck |
| Rung 1: Admin | $500–$700 | Stalled |
| Rung 2: Marketing | $750–$1,000 each | Friction |
| Rung 3: Sales | $3,500–$5,000+ | Freedom |
| Rung 4: Leadership | $15,000–$25,000+ | Flow |

### You Today: Stuck
You wear every hat: data entry, calling, follow-up, closing, admin.

### Rung 1: Admin — Data Manager ($500–$700/mo)
"Your cheapest task goes first. The Data Manager removes 3-4 hours of daily admin."

**Ownership Transferred:** Pull courthouse data, skip trace records, Trestle phone scoring, mail house coordination, CRM filter management, database hygiene.

**You Still Handle:** Make 150+ calls/day, send texts and voicemails, qualify leads, comp properties, make offers and negotiate.

### Rung 2: Marketing — Callers/Prospectors ($750–$1,000/mo each)
"You need leads or nothing else matters."

**Ownership Transferred:** All outbound calling (150+ dials/day per caller), voicemails and texts, initial appointment setting.

**You Still Handle:** Qualify leads and manage follow-up, comp properties, make offers, strategy.

### Rung 3: Sales — Lead Manager + Closer ($3,500–$5,000+/mo)
"You feel freedom because you only review deals and make high-level decisions."

**Ownership Transferred:** Lead qualification (LM), follow-up cadences (LM), appointment booking (LM), push-back loop (LM), deal negotiation and offers (Closer), comp analysis (Closer).

**You Still Handle:** Final deal approval on large transactions, strategy and growth decisions, team performance review.

### Rung 4: Leadership — Sales Mgr + Ops Mgr ($15,000–$25,000+/mo)
"You feel flow because the business operates without you in the day-to-day."

**You Focus On:** Vision and growth strategy, high-value relationships, market selection and expansion, capital allocation.

### Blueprint B Compression
"**Blueprint B compresses the ladder.** If you have a W-2 job and cannot answer the phone 9-5, you hire Data Manager and Lead Manager simultaneously in Week 1. You do not skip the Admin rung. You compress it. The Data Manager feeds clean, scored lists into the CRM while the Lead Manager answers calls. Without both, one hire sits idle."

## Blueprint Variations (Hiring Order)

### Blueprint A — Launchpad: Lean, Sweat-Equity
**First Hire:** Data Manager ($500-$700/mo)
1. Data Manager ($500-$700/mo): stop pulling data
2. Caller ($750-$1,000/mo): when you can't keep up with 150+ dials and closing
3. Lead Manager ($1,500-$3,000/mo): when inbound leads overflow your capacity

### Blueprint B — Optimizer: Time-Poor, Capital-Rich
**First Hires:** Data Manager + Lead Manager (simultaneous)
1. Data Manager ($500-$700/mo) + Lead Manager ($2,000-$3,000/mo): Week 1
2. 2 Callers ($750-$1,000/mo each): 2:1 ratio per LM, Week 3-4
3. Closer ($2,000 + commission): when 2 LMs booking consistent appointments
4. Sales Manager ($5,000-$6,000 + override): when team needs coaching at scale

### Blueprint C — Specialist: Deep-Research, High-Margin
**First Hire:** Data Manager ($500-$700/mo)
1. Data Manager ($500-$700/mo): deep prospecting research at scale
2. You do all calling (click-to-dial via smrtPhone, low volume, high personalization)
3. Lead Manager (optional): only if niche volume exceeds your capacity

### Blueprint D — Scale-Up: Process, Predictability
**First Hire:** Data Manager ($500-$700/mo)
1. Data Manager ($500-$700/mo): foundation before everything
2. Multiple Callers ($750-$1,000/mo each): 150+ dials/day per caller
3. Lead Manager(s) ($1,500-$3,000/mo each): 2 Callers per 1 LM
4. Closer ($2,000 + commission): when LMs booking consistent flow
5. Sales Manager ($5,000-$6,000 + override): manages growing sales team
6. Operations Manager ($5,000-$7,000 + bonus): removes you from day-to-day

## Information Flow (Handoffs)

Pipeline: **Data Manager** → Scored Lists → **Callers** → Warm Leads → **Lead Manager** → Qualified Appts → **Closer** → Signed Deals. Sales Mgr / Ops Mgr provide oversight. Push-back loop returns ghosted leads.

### Data Manager Handoff
Delivers scored, segmented call lists. Includes: property address, owner name, 3 phone numbers ranked by Trestle score, property type tag, attempt count. Mailing list to mail house same day for niche.

### Caller Handoff
Routes warm leads to Lead Manager with: contact name, phone, brief notes, initial interest level, property details. **Same-day handoff. "Speed to contact matters: Harvard study shows 400% higher conversion within the first minute."**

### Lead Manager Handoff
Books qualified appointments with Closer including: 4 Pillars score, seller's price expectations, property condition notes, motivation level. Hot leads (2+ pillars) → Closer. Cold/warm stay in LM cadence.

### Closer Output
Delivers signed contracts. Feedback flows back: which lead sources produce closeable deals, which property types convert, which objections recur.

## Glossary

- **Data Manager** — First hire for most blueprints. $500-$700/mo from Philippines.
- **Prospector / Caller** — 150+ dials/day. $750-$1,000/mo from Latin America.
- **Lead Manager** — 4 Pillars qualification. 50 attempts/day. $1,500-$3,000/mo.
- **Closer** — Consultative. $2,000 base + 3-10% commission. Ratio: 2 LM per 1 Closer.
- **Push-Back Loop** — Ghosted leads returned to LM cadence with temperature preserved. **20-30% of deals come from this loop.**
- **4 Pillars of Motivation** — Reason, Timeline, Condition, Price. 2+ pillars = hot, 1 = warm, 0 = cold.
- **Replacement Ladder** — 4-rung hiring framework: Admin, Marketing, Sales, Leadership. Stuck → Stalled → Friction → Freedom → Flow. Blueprint B compresses Rungs 1 and 3.
- **Trestle Phone Score** — 0-100. Dial First (81-100), Dial Second (61-80), Dial Third (41-60), Dial Fourth (21-40), Drop (0-20).
