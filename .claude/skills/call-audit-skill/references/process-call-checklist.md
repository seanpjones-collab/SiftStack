# Process Call Checklist — Evaluation Criteria

**Use for:** Cold Call, Follow-Up Call, Process Call, Manager Follow-Up

---

## Checklist Steps & Scoring

### SECTION 1: Intro (0–10)
- Did the specialist introduce themselves clearly and professionally?
- Was the opener confident and warm — not robotic or nervous?
- Did they make a quick human connection before diving into business?

---

### SECTION 2: Set Expectations — Opening (0–10)
All 4 (or 5) parts must be hit:

**a. Time** — Did they ask how much time the seller has or set a time boundary upfront?
**b. Agenda** — Did they explain what the call will cover ("Here's what I'd like to do today…")?
**c. Result** — Did they state what the outcome of the call will be ("By the end of this call we'll know if we can help you…")?
**d. Permission to Say No** — Did they explicitly give the seller permission to say no / remove pressure? ("If at any point this doesn't feel right, just say so…")
**e. Urgency** — Did they establish or probe for urgency early?

---

### SECTION 3: Motivation (0–10)
Evaluate depth and quality of motivation discovery. Should go at minimum 2–3 levels deep.

**a. Situation** — Did they uncover WHY the seller is selling? (The surface story)
**b. Impact 1** — Did they dig into the pain? First impact question. ("How has that been affecting you?")
**c. Impact 2** — Did they go one level deeper? ("What happens if this doesn't get resolved?")
**d. Impact 3** — Third level (not always required but highly valued). ("What would it mean for you if you could move past this?")
**e. Perfect Picture / Goal** — Did they ask what a perfect outcome looks like for the seller?

**Motivation categories to listen for (from The Motivation Categories doc):**
Financial pain, emotional pain, relationship/family pain, physical pain (property condition), mental fatigue, time burden, blocked goals, extreme pleasure (what they're moving toward), or no motivation (red flag).

**Key: Did the specialist identify whether the seller is moving AWAY FROM PAIN or TOWARD PLEASURE?**

---

### SECTION 4: Property Condition (0–10)
- Did they gather enough detail to estimate repairs?
- Did they ask about: overall condition, major systems (roof age, HVAC, HWH), foundation type (slab/crawl), well/septic, any structural or code issues?
- Did they use property condition info to inform the solution they'll offer?

---

### SECTION 5: Roadblocks (0–10)
All 4 parts should be uncovered:

**a. Time/Timeline** — Did they uncover WHEN the seller needs/wants to sell? Urgency?
**b. Influencers** — Did they ask about and identify all decision-makers?
  - Spouse/significant other
  - Family members (especially in probate/inheritance situations)
  - Attorney
  - Realtor
  - *Script: "Have you spoken with [them] about the idea of selling?"*
**c. Discomfort** — Did they surface any hesitations, concerns, or objections about the process itself?
**d. Money** — Did they get a ballpark number / what the seller needs to walk away with?
  - Should ask 3 times in 3 ways if seller won't give a number (per Rebuttal Sheet)

---

### SECTION 6: Set Expectations — Close / Next Steps (0–10)
All 4 parts must be hit to properly set up the offer call:

**a. Time** — Did they commit the seller to a specific time for the next call?
**b. Agenda** — Did they explain what the next call (offer call) will cover?
**c. Result** — Did they confirm what the outcome of the offer call will be?
**d. Permission to Say No** — Did they again give the seller permission to say no on the next call?

---

### UMBC Quick Check (bonus evaluation layer)
From The UMBC Checklist — did the specialist establish:
- **U — Urgency**: Yes / No / Solution presented for "No"
- **M — Motivation**: Distressed / Not Distressed
- **B — Ballpark**: Works (Qualified) / Doesn't Work
- **C — Condition**: Livable / Not Livable

---

### CHAMP Qualification Score (from ICP doc)
Did the specialist gather enough info to score the lead?

| Category | 0 pts (No Fit) | 1 pt (Workable) | 2 pts (Complete) |
|---|---|---|---|
| Challenges | No challenges, good condition | Some challenges, minor repairs | Clear challenges, major distress |
| Authority | Can't sell, not influencer | Some authority / influencer | Full authority, decision-makers present |
| Money | No equity | ~25%+ equity | 50%+ equity / fully equitized |
| Priority | No need to sell | Wants to sell within 90 days | Needs to sell within 30 days |

**0–2 = Dead/Cold. 3–4 = Warm (possible fit). 5–6 = Hot (probable fit). 7–8 = A-List.**

---

### Overall Call Score
Weighted average of Sections 1–6. Weight Motivation and Roadblocks more heavily (they are the core of the process call).
