# Common Office Names by Data Type

This reference helps identify the correct office when researching county data sources. Office names vary by jurisdiction—use these as search starting points.

## Probate Records

**Common office names:**
- Probate Court Clerk
- Surrogate's Court (NY, NJ)
- Orphans' Court (PA, MD, DE)
- Register of Wills
- Probate Division, Superior Court
- County Clerk - Probate Section

**What to search:**
- "[County] probate court"
- "[County] surrogate court"
- "[County] register of wills"

## State-Specific Variations

### Judicial vs Non-Judicial Foreclosure States

**Judicial foreclosure** (court-supervised): Look for lis pendens at courthouse
- FL, NY, NJ, IL, OH, PA, etc.

**Non-judicial foreclosure** (trustee sale): Look for Notice of Default at recorder
- TX, CA, AZ, GA, TN, etc.

### Deed of Trust vs Mortgage States

**Deed of Trust states**: Trustee handles foreclosure
- TX, CA, AZ, CO, VA, etc.

**Mortgage states**: Court handles foreclosure
- NY, FL, OH, IL, etc.

### Tax Sale Types

**Tax Lien Sale**: County sells lien, owner has redemption period
- AZ, FL, NJ, etc.

**Tax Deed Sale**: County sells property directly
- CA, GA, TX, etc.

**Redeemable Deed Sale**: Hybrid approach
- AL, TN, etc.
